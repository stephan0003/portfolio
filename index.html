Fun Projects

Thermoelectric car Project @ProjectLink[do not click]

The thermoelectric generator challenge project consists of building a vehicle using 4 main components, a Peltier device, a DC servo motor, and a single wick heat source or a tea light candle in this case and a cold source. The vehicle must complete two key challenges to successfully beat the challenge. It must first travel a one-meter strip and then climb a 36” long incline ramp at a 20o angle. The vehicle runs as follows: A heat source and a cold source are set on each side of the Peltier device. The higher the temperature differential between the faces of the Peltier device, the higher the voltage generated will be to run the motor. After a lot of testing, a simple solution was determined. Our vehicle runs a typical rear-wheel-drive setup. An ice bath on top, and candle on the bottom sandwich the Peltier device. This setup generates a consistent 2V which gets our vehicle to move all the way to the top of the ramp.

Hovercraft Project @ProjectLink[do not click]

The comparison of the three separate preliminary designs for a miniature hovercraft will be analyzed. Only one of the three designs will be proposed to be used in the final design. The hovercraft must be able to autonomously navigate a circuit while hovering at least 3 centimeters off of the ground. The selected design was chosen on the SWOT, WOT, and AHP analysis between the three designs. Certain criteria were given different levels of priority depending on what the team believed will achieve the goals of the hovercraft to complete the circuit. The selected design also respects all specifications and requirements that have been defined by the outline of this project. The selected design was picked by the team based on calculations, testing and simulation with the use of matlab. The project itself will also be presented under the point of view of the schedule, illustrating all tasks that were completed and how they relate to one another; aiding the team to better work around deadlines. The risk management and contingency presents the main problems that occurred during the project with the hovercraft and how the team managed to solve the issues.
